Bem-vindo(a) à Minha Página Pessoal

MSc student at the Laboratory of Theoretical and Computational Physics, researching emergent gravity. My academic interests include modified gravity theories, the holographic principle, and the AdS/CFT correspondence.

I hold a degree in Computational Mathematics from the State University of Campinas (Unicamp). Beyond theoretical physics, I have a strong interest in education, science communication, and the philosophy of science.

For over ten years, I have been part of GPEC (Group for Research and Science Education), a science outreach initiative where I teach the wonders of the universe to general audiences, encouraging critical thinking through experiments and sky observations.

I also curate a science fiction film club at the Museum of Image and Sound in Campinas, São Paulo, where I organize themed screenings to explore how science and technology engage with societal issues and inspire solutions.
